Our Philosophy

More than a cut — a ritual for the discerning

Treysson Trimz, is a modern, premium barbershop brand redefining the grooming experience in Nairobi. Positioned as more than just a haircut destination, it delivers a lifestyle-driven “man cave” experience where style, comfort, and confidence meet. At Treysson Trimz, we believe grooming is more than a routine—it’s an experience. Built for the modern man (and anyone who values looking sharp), we’ve created a space where style, comfort, and confidence come together. We are not just a barbershop—we are a full-service grooming and wellness hub. From clean, precise haircuts to expert dreadlock services and nail tech, we also offer professional massage therapy—including Swedish and deep tissue—designed to help you relax, recover, and recharge. In addition, our salon services ensure that every client, male or female, can enjoy top-tier beauty and grooming care under one roof. Our signature touch? A relaxing, man cave-inspired environment paired with thoughtful extras like complimentary mini-massages to elevate every visit.

Located in Utawala along Kinka/Airways Road, Treysson Trimz serves a vibrant, style-conscious community that expects more than just a service—they expect an experience. Bold, fresh, and customer-first, Treysson Trimz is redefining grooming and wellness—one sharp look and one great experience at a time. Reach Us @TreyssonTrimz_utawala on all social media platforms.
